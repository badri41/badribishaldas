C++: My Long-Term Relationship with Compile Errors

C++ / Systems / Concurrency New

Why this blog exists

C++ is the only language that can make you feel like a genius at 2:00 PM and emotionally unemployed by 2:07 PM. I love it anyway.

I am learning C++ in the most honest way possible: by writing code, getting humbled, watching talks, and then pretending I always knew that undefined behavior was the issue.

What this means in practice

I am trying to balance cool advanced topics with proper basics. So yes, I watch atomics and coroutine talks, then immediately return to Back to Basics like a responsible adult who has been humbled by one race condition.

Current state

I used to think templates were scary. Now I think templates are normal, and linker errors are scary. This is growth.

Goal for the next few weeks: build stronger basics, write cleaner concurrent code, and make peace with the fact that C++ is both a language and a lifestyle.
